Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the mood detection?

Our AI uses advanced sentiment analysis with high accuracy. It analyzes text patterns, keywords, and context to understand your emotional state.

Is Mood Music free to use?

Yes! Mood Music is completely free. You can discover unlimited music recommendations without any cost.

Do I need a Spotify account?

While you don't need an account to get recommendations, having Spotify allows you to play songs directly through our embedded players.

Can I suggest new features?
